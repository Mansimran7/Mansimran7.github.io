My Story

I have always been a tech enthusiast, primarily because of the fact I was introduced to coding and algorithms from early school days. From then on I knew for a fact that this is where I wanted to grow.

For my undergrad I got inducted in VIT, Vellore in the Computer Science program and was fortunate to be part of an immense cosmopolitan atmosphere at campus. During my college I was lucky to meet like-minded people working with whom I have been able to hone and enhance my technological skills as well as organize national level technical conferences.

I have contributed to the open source community whilst my fellowship at IIT-Bombay and also have been an avid social worker while volunteering for Make a differnece (one of the largest NGOs in India). During the final year of my college I got placed in UBS where I am currenty working on buiding models that will in coming future be used to facilitate easy onboarding of clients.
